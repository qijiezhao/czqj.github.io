Biography
I am a second-year graduate student in the Institute of Computer Science&Technology, Peking University, advised by Yongtao Wang. I received my B.Eng from School of Computer and Information at Hefei University of Technology(HFUT).
 
Research interests
My research interests include: Computer Vision, Deep Learning, Object Detection, Semantic Segmentation, Action Recognition, Transfer learning, Big Data.
